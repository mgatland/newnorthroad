The Pop-Up Games Arcade

Time: Saturday, 2 November, 11 AM to 4 PM.
Place: The Bread and Butter Letter's basement, 225 Karangahape Road.
How much: $5 cash at the door.

More people play games than ever before, but few Aucklanders know about the DIY game makers in their own city.

On November 2nd, the Pop-Up Games Arcade will appear with 10 indie games made by local creatives.

The arcade's purpose is to grow a community around local games, the same way that people get together to support local bands, painters, poets and zinesters.

The 10 games were chosen from over 100 made in Auckland over the last few years. Some are new, unreleased games; others are older games that work great in a social setting. Most of them are very strange, but should be easy to play even if you're new to indie games.

Built from cardboard, paint and old computers, the arcade is colourful and messy more like an art class than a computer lab.

All ages are welcome. A few of the games have mature themes.

more info at http://newnorthroad.com/arcade/
